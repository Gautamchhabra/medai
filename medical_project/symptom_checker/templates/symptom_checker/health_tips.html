Healthy Snacking

Replace chips and biscuits with nuts, seeds, fruits, or yogurt. They provide energy and essential nutrients.

Portion Control

Use smaller plates and bowls. Eat slowly and stop when you feel 80% full to avoid overeating.

Regular Exercise

Get at least 30 minutes of moderate physical activity 5 days a week. Brisk walking, cycling, or swimming are great options.

Fitness
🚶

Take the Stairs

Skip the elevator for short distances. Walking up stairs burns calories and strengthens your leg muscles.

Fitness
🧘

Morning Stretching

Spend 10 minutes stretching every morning. It improves flexibility, reduces stiffness, and boosts blood circulation.

🧘

Practice Meditation

Even 5-10 minutes of daily meditation can reduce stress, improve focus, and enhance emotional well-being.

Mental Health
📖

Read Daily

Reading for 20 minutes a day stimulates your brain, reduces stress, and improves concentration and vocabulary.

Mental Health
👥

Social Connection

Spend quality time with family and friends. Strong social connections boost happiness and mental health.

Hand Washing

Wash hands with soap for at least 20 seconds, especially before eating, after using the restroom, and after being outside.

Hygiene

Oral Hygiene

Brush twice daily for 2 minutes and floss once a day. Good oral health prevents many systemic diseases.

Schedule regular health check-ups even when you feel fine. Early detection saves lives.
