💊 Common Medicines Guide

Information about commonly used medicines in India

⚠️ Educational Purpose Only - Not Medical Advice
💊
Paracetamol (Acetaminophen)
Analgesic / Antipyretic
Fever & Pain
Uses: Fever, headache, body ache, mild pain
Dosage: 500mg-650mg every 4-6 hours (max 4g/day)
Caution: Avoid with liver disease. Do not exceed dose.
Crocin Dolo 650 Calpol Pacimol
💢
Ibuprofen
NSAID (Non-Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory)
Pain & Inflammation
Uses: Inflammation, dental pain, menstrual cramps, arthritis
Dosage: 200-400mg every 6-8 hours
Caution: Take with food. Avoid in stomach ulcers, kidney issues.
Brufen Ibugesic Combiflam
🤧
Cetirizine
Antihistamine (2nd generation)
Allergy & Cold
Uses: Allergies, runny nose, sneezing, itching, hives
Dosage: 10mg once daily
Caution: May cause mild drowsiness. Avoid alcohol.
Zyrtec Cetzine Alerid Okacet
🤢
Antacid (Aluminum Hydroxide + Magnesium)
Antacid / Anti-Gas
Stomach
Uses: Acidity, heartburn, gas, indigestion
Dosage: 10-20ml after meals or as needed
Caution: Take 2 hrs apart from other medicines.
Digene Gelusil Eno Mucaine
💧
ORS (Oral Rehydration Salts)
Electrolyte Replacement
Stomach
Uses: Dehydration from diarrhea, vomiting, heat exhaustion
Dosage: 1 packet in 1 liter clean water, sip throughout day
Caution: Use clean drinking water. Discard after 24 hours.
Electral ORS-L Walyte
🍊
Vitamin C (Ascorbic Acid)
Vitamin Supplement / Antioxidant
Vitamins
Uses: Immunity boost, wound healing, iron absorption
Dosage: 500mg-1000mg daily
Caution: High doses may cause stomach upset.
Limcee Celimax C-Lix
🩹
Clotrimazole Cream
Topical Antifungal
Skin Care
Uses: Fungal skin infections, ringworm, athlete's foot
Application: Apply thin layer 2-3 times daily for 2-4 weeks
Caution: For external use only. Complete full course.
Candid Surfaz Clotrim
