Basic First Aid Tips

🩹 Minor Cuts & Scrapes

Clean with water, apply antiseptic cream, cover with sterile bandage. Change dressing daily.

🔥 Minor Burns

Run cool water for 10-15 minutes. Do NOT apply ice. Cover with sterile gauze. No butter or oil.

🦴 Sprains

Follow RICE: Rest, Ice (20 min), Compression (elastic bandage), Elevation above heart level.

😵 Fainting

Lay person flat, elevate legs. Loosen tight clothing. If not breathing, start CPR and call 108.

🤒 High Fever

Remove excess clothing, give paracetamol, use cool compress on forehead. Seek medical help if above 104°F.

🐍 Snake Bite

Stay calm, immobilize bitten limb, remove jewelry near bite. Do NOT suck venom. Rush to hospital immediately.
