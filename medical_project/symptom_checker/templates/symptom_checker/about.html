MediAI

AI-Powered Health Assistant for Educational Purposes

Version 1.0 • Educational Project

About This Project

MediAI is an educational project designed to demonstrate how artificial intelligence can be used to provide general health information. This application uses advanced AI models to analyze user-described symptoms and provide educational insights about possible conditions, general precautions, and commonly prescribed medications.

This project was built as a learning exercise to explore the integration of AI APIs with web applications. It showcases features like user authentication, symptom analysis, health history tracking, emergency contact management, and a comprehensive medicine information database.

Important Disclaimer

  • Not a Medical Device: This application is NOT a medical device and has not been evaluated by any medical regulatory authority including the FDA, CDSCO (India), or any other health agency.
  • Not Medical Advice: The information provided by this application is for educational and informational purposes only. It does NOT constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
  • Consult a Doctor: Always consult a licensed healthcare professional for proper diagnosis and treatment of any medical condition. Do not delay seeking medical help based on information from this app.
  • No Self-Medication: Do NOT self-medicate based on the medication suggestions provided. Medications should only be taken under the guidance of a qualified doctor.
  • AI Limitations: The AI model may produce inaccurate or incomplete information. Results should be verified with professional medical sources.
  • Emergency: In case of a medical emergency, call 108 (India) or your local emergency number immediately. Do not rely on this application for emergency situations.
